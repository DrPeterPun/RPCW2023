Discover Honduras

About Honduras

Honduras is a beautiful country located in Central America.

Attractions

Copán Ruins

The Copán Ruins are an archaeological site of the ancient Mayan civilization and a UNESCO World Heritage site. The site features impressive stone temples, hieroglyphic staircases, and intricately carved stelae. It is a must-visit destination for history and archaeology enthusiasts.

Roatán

Roatán is a tropical paradise located in the Caribbean Sea. It is part of the Bay Islands and offers beautiful white sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant coral reefs. Roatán is a popular destination for diving, snorkeling, and relaxation.

Cayos Cochinos

Cayos Cochinos is an archipelago consisting of two small islands and 13 coral cays. It is a protected marine area known for its pristine beauty and rich biodiversity. Visitors can enjoy snorkeling, swimming, and exploring the unspoiled natural surroundings.

Traditional Cuisine

Honduran cuisine reflects the countrys diverse cultural influences. Some popular Honduran dishes include baleadas, which are flour tortillas filled with beans, cheese, and other toppings, plátanos fritos (fried plantains), and sopa de caracol (conch soup), a traditional seafood dish.
